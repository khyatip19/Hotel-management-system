Conveniently located at just 0.6 mi away from Ferry Station, Helipad and Sea plane Terminal, Seascape resort, Andaman offers comfortable and cozy accommodations. It features a restaurant and bar.
The serene beach of Kalapathar is located 3.1 mi away while the unique Elephant Beach is located 3.7 mi away. The pristine Radha Nagar Beach is 5 mi away.
Attached with a private balcony and offering a sea view, the air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a mini-bar, electric kettle and flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathrooms come with hot/cold shower.
Guests can sample a variety of Indian and International cuisines from the in-house restaurant, Urban Takka. Refreshing beverages are served in Fluidz, the in-house bar.
