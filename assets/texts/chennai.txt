Seascape resort,Chennai offers 4-star rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite flat-screen TVs located one kilometer from Marina Beach. Offering free parking, it features a spa, fitness center and outdoor pool.
Along with classic wood furnishings, our air-conditioned rooms feature neutral fabrics and a safe. For convenience, a minibar and free tea/coffee-making amenities are provided. Private bathrooms have hot/cold showers and a hairdryer.
With trained professionals at hand, all-round workouts can be done at the well-equipped fitness center. Relaxing massages and body treatments await at the spa. A tour desk and business center are available.
